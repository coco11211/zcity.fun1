Server Rules

I. In-Game Rules

  • Ignorance of the rules is no excuse.
  • Using cheats (third-party software that allows you to gain an advantage over other players) is punishable by a permanent ban.
  • NSFW, NSFL, Nazi sprays are punishable by a permanent ban.
  • If you find a bug, please report it in the dedicated Discord channel. If you don't want it to be seen, you can create a ticket. If you exploit the bug or keep it out of our sight intentionally, you may be temporarily (1-7 days) banned from the server.
  • Metagaming is not allowed and can be punished by a temporary (3-7 days) ban.
  • Don't be a dick, treat every other player with respect. You might get temporarily (3-14 days) muted/gagged if you go too far.

II. In-Game Chat Rules

  • Incitement to interethnic conflicts in chat or voice chat is punishable by a (3 days/permanent) mute/gag.
    • Nazi statements.
    • Hard-r n-words.
    • Any other insults based on national characteristics.
  • This is a game server, don't discuss politics here, and if you incite others to do so, you could get your communications blocked (1-7 days) on the server.
  • Praising known terrorists or acts of terrorism will result in a permanent ban.
